Data Collection and Usage

Nature42 is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains how we handle your data.

AI/LLM Data Usage

Nature42 uses AI language models (via the Strands Agent SDK and Amazon Bedrock) to generate game content including:

  • Location descriptions and narratives
  • NPC dialogue and interactions
  • Puzzle generation and evaluation
  • Location imagery

Your commands and game interactions are sent to these AI services to generate responses. This data is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently by the AI service beyond what is necessary for content generation.

Browser Storage

Nature42 stores your game progress locally in your browser using IndexedDB. This includes:

  • Your current location and inventory
  • Keys collected and puzzle progress
  • Generated location descriptions and world data
  • NPC interaction history
  • Your decision history within the game

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except when necessary to generate new content.

Data Retention

Game state data is retained in your browser until you:

  • Clear your browser data/cache
  • Start a new game (which overwrites the previous save)
  • Manually delete the saved game

We do not maintain server-side copies of your game progress.

Age Requirements

Nature42 is designed for players aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

Nature42 uses Amazon Bedrock (via Strands Agent SDK) for AI content generation. Please refer to Amazon's privacy policy for information about how they handle data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of Nature42 after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the project repository.
